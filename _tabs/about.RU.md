Hello everyone! My name is Bogdan, and here you’ll find my personal blog.

I started it because I want to indulge my graphomania, because I get lonely sometimes, and simply — why not. I’d like to keep things more or less on the intellectual side, but we’ll see how it goes. Probably I should begin with the following.

> Abandon hope all ye who enter here.
> 
> I do not intend to offend anyone. I condemn everything bad and support everything good! Also, regarding the blog’s visuals: I can’t draw, and I don’t want to spend money on design, so sometimes the media content, including banners and covers, is the result of magical neural matrix multiplication. I also use AI to fix imperfections in photos when sharing pictures of people I know. BUT! The texts are my own work — otherwise the blog would be meaningless. The music published under my name is also the result of creative activity of **living** neurons.
{: .prompt-danger }

My native language is Russian. Back in school, I studied math and physics, so I neglected most humanities subjects, including English — and I still feel the consequences of that. So if possible, please keep in mind that you’re reading someone who isn’t even from Eastern Europe. Some posts may appear in Russian or German or Whatever — it all depends on the topic and my mood.

> Achtung! 
> 
> This site is primarily designed for desktop users. Mobile and laptop versions may display reduced content.
{: .prompt-warning }

If you need to reach me for any reason, feel free to email me or message me on Telegram.
